Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four rupees below. By click one one you will add a specific amount to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
